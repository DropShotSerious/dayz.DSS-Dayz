Server Rules

Welcome to DSS The Oh S*** Server! We are excited to have you in our community. To ensure a fun and fair experience for everyone, we have a few rules that we ask all players to follow:

General Rules

  • No Cheating or Exploiting: Any form of cheating, exploiting glitches, or using third-party software is strictly prohibited.
  • Respect All Players: Treat everyone with respect. Toxic behavior and harassment will not be tolerated.
  • No Griefing: Do not intentionally ruin the experience for others by destroying their hard work or sabotaging their gameplay.
  • Respect the Event Guidelines: Follow specific event rules to ensure a fun and fair competition for all participants.
  • Stay Active: If you're not playing for an extended period, please inform an admin or staff member. Inactive players may be removed.
  • Play Fair: Our server is about a fun and challenging experience, and everyone deserves a fair chance. No "pay-to-win" advantages allowed.

Event Rules

  • Event Participation: Players must follow event-specific instructions to participate in tournaments or challenges.
  • Sportsmanship: While competitive, events should be fun. No toxicity or bad sportsmanship will be tolerated.
  • No Exploits: During events, no cheating or exploiting of event mechanics. Violation of this will result in disqualification.

Consequences of Breaking Rules

We take rule-breaking seriously. Players who violate these rules will face consequences ranging from temporary bans to permanent removal from the server, depending on the severity of the infraction.
